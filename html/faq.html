FAQs

How does the Medicinal Plant Identification Tool work?
Our tool uses deep learning algorithms to analyze the input image and predicts the most probable class.

What types of images can I upload for plant identification?
You can upload photos of medicinal plant and or leaves. Clear and well-lit images generally yield better results.

Is the identification process instantaneous?
Identification typically takes only a few seconds, but it may vary depending on the image's complexity and the server's load.

What information will I receive after plant identification?
You'll receive the common name, scientific name, and brief information about the identified medicinal plant. We also provide links for more in-depth information.

Is the identification process accurate?
While we strive for high accuracy, no automated system is perfect. Our tool is constantly improving, and user feedback helps enhance its performance.
